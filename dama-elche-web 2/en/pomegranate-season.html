Pomegranate season: what to expect this year

Season calendar, varieties and calibre and quality forecasts.

23 Apr 2025

Every pomegranate season is different, but the excitement is always the same: bringing to market a fruit at its perfect point. Here is what to expect from the season and how to plan your orders.

Season calendar

The harvest of the Mollar pomegranate from Elche begins in early autumn and runs well into winter. Earlier varieties such as the Acco arrive first, while the Wonderful brings colour and intensity to the middle of the campaign.

Varieties and sizes

  • Mollar de Elche: sweet, tender seed, ideal for fresh consumption.
  • Wonderful: intense colour and flavour with character.
  • Acco: early variety, opens the season.

Calibre and quality forecasts depend on the year's weather, which is why we monitor the fields closely to fine-tune volumes and availability.
