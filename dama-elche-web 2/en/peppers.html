Distinctive features

  • California and Lamuyo varieties in red, yellow, orange and green, with calibres for retail and HORECA.
  • Firm flesh, glossy skin and excellent commercial presentation that reduces waste.
  • Balanced flavour, crunchy texture and high post-harvest performance.
  • Extended cold-store life with no loss of firmness.
  • Graded to European "Extra" or "I" quality standards.

Seasonal availability

The main season runs from May to October across protected and open-air crops in south-east Spain. In winter we keep supply going with greenhouse production, ensuring availability nearly all year for retail, HORECA and fresh-cut.

Growing conditions

  • Mild, stable climate, optimal between 18–30 °C.
  • Well-structured sandy-loam soils, slightly acidic pH and good aeration.
  • Drip irrigation with moisture sensors to avoid water stress.
  • Fertigation tuned to the crop stage and leaf analysis.

Sustainable practices

  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM) with beneficial fauna and sticky traps.
  • Natural biostimulants (seaweed extracts).
  • Ground cover between rows to reduce erosion.
  • GlobalG.A.P. and GRASP certification.
