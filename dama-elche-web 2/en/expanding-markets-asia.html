Frutas Dama de Elche expands into Asian markets

New distribution deals bring our fruit to strategic markets.

25 Apr 2025

Frutas Dama de Elche's commitment to quality and traceability is opening new doors. Over recent seasons we have strengthened our international presence with new distribution agreements that bring our fruit to strategic markets across Asia.

Quality that travels far

Exporting fresh fruit thousands of kilometres demands control at every link. Our cold chain, selection protocols and packaging designed for long-distance transport ensure that pomegranates, peppers and the rest of our categories arrive flawless at their destination.

Certifications that open markets

Holding international standards such as GlobalG.A.P., IFS and BRCGS is not just a guarantee for the consumer: it is the key that lets us work with the most demanding retail in each country.

We keep growing sustainably, hand in hand with our growers and focused on bringing the flavour of the Mediterranean to ever more corners of the world.
