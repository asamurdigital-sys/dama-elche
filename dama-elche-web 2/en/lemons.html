Distinctive features

  • Fino (Primofiori), Verna, Eureka and Lisbon varieties, selected for calibre and juice quality.
  • Thin, aromatic skin, rich in essential oils.
  • High juice content and balanced acidity.
  • Harvested at the optimal point and graded by calibre.
  • Grown with full traceability and GlobalG.A.P. certification.

Seasonal availability

The Fino variety is harvested from October to February and the Verna from February to July, letting us offer quality Spanish lemon almost all year round. Varietal planning ensures continuous supply for retail, industry and export.

Health benefits

  • Immune support thanks to vitamin C.
  • Antioxidant and alkalising effect.
  • Aids digestion and iron absorption.

Benefits are associated with regular consumption within a balanced diet. Not a substitute for medical advice.

Growing conditions

  • Warm Mediterranean climate with mild winters (the lemon tree is frost-sensitive).
  • Well-drained, sandy-loam, slightly acidic soils.
  • Localised drip irrigation with moisture control.
  • Staggered flowering and fruit set allowing several harvests.

Sustainable practices

  • Efficient irrigation with climate sensors in a low-rainfall area.
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and biological control.
  • Ground cover and organic matter for the soil.
  • GlobalG.A.P. and GRASP certification.
